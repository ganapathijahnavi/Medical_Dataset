# Alkaline Phosphatase (ALP)

**Normal Range:**  
44 - 147 IU/L

**Clinical Significance:**  
Enzyme related to liver, bone, and bile duct function.

**Future Diseases:**  
- Liver disease  
- Bone disorders  
- Biliary obstruction

**Lifestyle Recommendations:**  
- Monitor liver enzymes  
- Adequate calcium and vitamin D intake  
- Regular check-ups
