# Alanine Aminotransferase (ALT)

**Normal Range:**  
7 - 56 IU/L

**Clinical Significance:**  
Liver enzyme; elevated in liver damage.

**Future Diseases:**  
- Hepatitis  
- Fatty liver disease  
- Cirrhosis

**Lifestyle Recommendations:**  
- Limit alcohol  
- Healthy diet  
- Regular liver function monitoring
