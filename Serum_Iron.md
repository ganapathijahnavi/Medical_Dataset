# Serum Iron

**Normal Range:**  
- Men: 65 – 175 µg/dL  
- Women: 50 – 150 µg/dL  

**Clinical Significance:**  
Measures circulating iron bound to transferrin. Helps in evaluating anemia and iron metabolism.  

**Future Diseases:**  
- Iron-deficiency anemia  
- Hemochromatosis  
- Chronic disease anemia
