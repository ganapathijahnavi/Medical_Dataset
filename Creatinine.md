# Creatinine

**Normal Range:**  
Men: 0.74 - 1.35 mg/dL  
Women: 0.59 - 1.04 mg/dL

**Clinical Significance:**  
Indicator of kidney function.

**Future Diseases:**  
- Chronic kidney disease  
- Acute kidney injury

**Lifestyle Recommendations:**  
- Avoid nephrotoxic drugs  
- Maintain hydration  
- Monitor regularly if at risk
