# Blood Urea Nitrogen (BUN)

**Normal Range:**  
7 - 20 mg/dL

**Clinical Significance:**  
Measures kidney function and hydration.

**Future Diseases:**  
- Kidney disease  
- Heart failure  
- Dehydration

**Lifestyle Recommendations:**  
- Maintain hydration  
- Limit protein intake if kidney function impaired  
- Follow renal specialist advice
